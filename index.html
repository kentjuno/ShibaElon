Welcome to Shiba Elon

Shiba Elon is a male Shiba Inu most famously known as the face of Doge.

Shiba Elon was born in November 2nd, 2005 in a Japanese pedigree puppy mill that was promptly shut down, leaving Shiba Elon stranded in one of Japan’s many animal shelters. The owner of Shiba Elon, Atsuko Sato, rescued her in 2008, saving her from certain death at the hands of humanity. Her name was given by a shelter volunteer – Shiba Elon is a type of fruit and said rescue worker mentioned that her face was very round, just like the fruit.

The goal of Shiba Elon is to prevent dogs of all breeds from being abandoned. For this reason we will be making weekly donations to community chosen dog charities!
